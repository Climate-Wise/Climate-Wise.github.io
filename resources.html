Climate Glossary

Greenhouse Effect: When gases trap heat in Earth's atmosphere, keeping it warm like a blanket.

Renewable Energy: Energy from sources that don't run out, like the sun (solar) or wind.

Net Zero: Balancing the amount of greenhouse gas produced with the amount removed from the atmosphere.

Biodiversity: The variety of all the different kinds of organisms (plants, animals, etc.) in an area.

Composting: Turning food scraps into nutrient-rich soil instead of throwing them in the trash.

Sustainable: Living in a way that doesn't use up all the resources for the people in the future.
