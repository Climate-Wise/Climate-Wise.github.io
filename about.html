What is Global Warming?

Global warming is the rapid increase in Earth’s average surface temperature. It's not something that can disappear with a snap of your fingers. It causes wildfires, rising sea-levels, and droughts.

Who causes it?

It is largely driven by human activities like the burning of fossil fuels which releases greenhouse gases that trap heat in the atmosphere. Powering homes and businesses produces massive amounts of CO2.

Can we do anything?

Yes! We can switch to renewable sources (solar/wind), drive electric vehicles, and support sustainable local businesses. If we all work together, we can make a change.
